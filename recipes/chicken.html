Saucy Chicken

Description

This recipe is possibly the easiest and surely my favorite of the three. It takes around 1 hour to make

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • 2 peppers
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 spoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Salt
  • 1 teaspoon of colorant or saffron
  • 1/2 a glass of white wine
  • 12 to 16 chicken drumsticks

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables and fill the bottom of the pot with oil
  2. Throw in the pot all vegetables and stir-fry
  3. Add white wine, spices, and chicken drumsticks to the pot
  4. Optionally, add water to the pot
  5. Cook for at least 40 minutes and stir every once in a while
