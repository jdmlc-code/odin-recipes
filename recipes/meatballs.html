Meatballs with Almond Sauce

Description

This is a step by step recipe for meatballs with almond sauce. Fried potatoes or rice work quite well as a side dish for this.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • 1 onion
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 4 slices of stale bread
  • A little parsley
  • Breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg or 2
  • 1/2 a glass of white wine
  • Minced meat (prefereably from the butcher, beef and pork)

Steps

  1. In a pot, add the onion (roughly chopped, since it will be blended later), the bread slices and the almonds. Add 1 teaspoon of salt and some oil. Saut√© until softened.
  2. Once the onion is tender, remove everything from the pot and blend with a hand blender in a tall container. Return the blended mixture to the pot. Add the white wine and enough water to fill the pot to just below halfway.
  3. Keep the sauce simmering on low heat. In a bowl, mix the minced meat with 1 chopped garlic clove, parsley, nutmeg, and 2 teaspoons of salt. Add the egg. Mix with your hands, gradually adding breadcrumbs until the mixture no longer sticks to your hands.
  4. Increase the heat, form meatballs, and add them to the pot with the sauce. Cook for about 30 minutes, or longer if the sauce needs to get texture.
